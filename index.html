Experimental by design

A small playground for curious builds

Vanduo Labs is where we ship ideas before they are polished—interactive demos, odd widgets, and half-serious prototypes that might graduate into the framework, or might just make us smile. Most demos are not guaranteed stable; that is the point. Some components, like vd-neptune-search, are experimental prototypes that may graduate into the framework.

Try things

Grids, search, and UI experiments invite you to click, drag, and break assumptions safely. If something misbehaves, we have learned something useful.

Fun is a feature

We keep the tone light on purpose: exploration should feel inviting, not like reading a spec sheet. The GLaDOS jokes are optional; the curiosity is not.
